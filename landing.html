Summary

The purpose of this project was to analysis how weather patterns may change and alter as one travels closer to the equator.

The four dependent variables are: Max Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed. This dashboard shows the source data and the visualizations based on the four dependent variables.
